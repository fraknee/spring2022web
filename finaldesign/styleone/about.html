Our Mission

For the past 9 years, we’ve been a company with strong beliefs about the improvement of both people and planet. We believe that a company's values are as important as the products it makes. We also believe that we can save the environment through advancements in technology that actively work towards sustainabiltiy.

While we are aware that we cannot simply change large corporations to become eco-friendly in the snap of a finger, we believe that no matter how small the change is, it can and will contribute to a better planet. We’re using the resources we have—our business, our investments, our voice and our imaginations—to do something about it, and we hope to expand our products to all forms of transportation in the future.



How it Started

Co-founders Kash Olvido and Aimi Ashtyn were having their typical date night tradition of cooking dinner together when, midway through cooking the chicken parmigiana, Aimi asked out loud "What fuel do airplanes use?". She didn't really have any ideas in mind, but she did know that it probably created pollution. Kash replied with a thoughtful shrug, not knowing that that simple question would kickstart their next 9 years creating Flying Quatro Inc., a company focused on decreasing aviation polution.

After months and months of researching, Aimi and Kash knew they had a company on their hands. So after many, many test trials and a few more additions to the team, Flying Quatro Inc. was born! One of the leading corporations in the flying industry, we don't plan on stopping anytime soon.

Why Aviation?

As avid travelers, Aimi and Kash hope to create a path for others to travel more often without leaving such a negative footprint on the planet.

"We hope that 4Fuel will also allow airlines to lower their ticket fares. Traveling is a priviledge that we someday hope to make more accessible to all types of people."
- Kash Olvido
